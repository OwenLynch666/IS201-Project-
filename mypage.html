My Experience with the Sport



It was a beatiful warm evening in Provo Utah, when my family and I were hiking the Y. As we approached the top of the mountain and sat down to enjoy the view, we heard a sharp whistle coming from directly behind us. Before we could turn our heads 3 speedflyers flew past us faster then 60mph just above our heads. I turned to my dad and said "I'm doing that" That's wehre it all began.

With in a couple of weeks, I began training to learn to fly. I took lessons for weeks, to get my license, and I saved up my money, so I could buy my own wing. I instantly fell in love with being in the air, and I've been doing it now for 3 years. Speedflying is something that makes me feel so free and alive, and I can't imagine my life without it.
